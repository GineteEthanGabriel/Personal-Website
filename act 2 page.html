Gaming Is More Than Just Playing for Me

Introduction

After long days of classes and commuting, I often feel both physically and mentally exhausted. Because of this, I needed something that could help me relax and reset my mind. For me, that something is gaming.

Conclusion / Reflection

Gaming has become more than just a hobby for me, it’s a way to relax, cope with stress, and stay motivated. Taking breaks does not mean being lazy. It means taking care of myself so I can continue moving forward.
